    Hardware Requirements

    The following hardware specifications define the minimum and recommended requirements for running the Bull system efficiently.

    These requirements are designed to support stable operation, handle medium to high workloads, and ensure optimal performance during processing, deployment, and runtime. Systems with higher specifications may deliver improved responsiveness and scalability, especially in production environments.

    List of Requirements

    1. Operating system : CentOS 8
    2. Processor: Intel i7 to i9 (minimum 16 cores)
    3. Minimum Speed: 3.5 GHz (Recommended: above 4.0 GHz)
    4. RAM : 32GB (16GB for Medium Load)
    5. Storage: 500GB HDD or SSD (recommend SSD)
